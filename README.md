# Customer Churn Prediction Platform

## Problem Statement

Customer churn refers to the phenomenon where customers stop using a company's products or services over a period of time. It is one of the biggest challenges faced by businesses because acquiring a new customer is often much more expensive than retaining an existing one.

Customer churn prediction uses historical customer data and machine learning techniques to identify customers who are likely to leave in the near future. By identifying these customers early, companies can take proactive measures such as offering personalized discounts, improving customer support, or launching targeted marketing campaigns to retain them.

Churn prediction is widely used across multiple industries including:

- Telecommunications
- Banking and Financial Services
- Insurance
- E-commerce
- Subscription-based Businesses
- Online Streaming Platforms
- Software as a Service (SaaS)

The primary beneficiaries of churn prediction include business executives, marketing teams, customer relationship managers, sales teams, and customer support departments. These stakeholders use predictive insights to improve customer retention strategies, optimize marketing budgets, and increase customer lifetime value.

The business objective of this project is to develop an end-to-end machine learning platform capable of predicting whether a customer is likely to churn based on historical customer information. The platform will assist organizations in making data-driven decisions that reduce customer loss, improve customer satisfaction, and increase long-term profitability.
